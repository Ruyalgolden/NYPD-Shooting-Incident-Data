Datasets

Since 1845, The NYPD also known as the New York City Police Department, is the Police Force which serves and protects the boroughs of New York City. There are approximately 36,000 officers and 19,000 civilian employees. For the past 25 years, the NYPD department has achieved an amazing decline in both violence and crime making certain that New York has the lowest overall crime rate out of 25 large major cities.

The NYPD shooting incident data, is a list of every shooting incident that happened in New York, from 2006 all the way to the present day. All the data is manually checked and reviewed by the Office of Management Analysis and Planning, before being posted onto the list.

NYPD Shooting Incident Data
(Historic)

Data provided by the Police Department (NYPD) that breaks down every shooting incident that occurred in NYC from 2006 to the end of the previous calendar year.

Each record shows a shooting incident which includes information about the event such as the location and time of occurrence. Some records include information about the suspectand victim.

NYPD Shooting Incident Data
(Year To Date)

Data provided by the Police Department (NYPD) that breaks down every shooting incident that occurred in NYC this current year.

Each record shows a shooting incident which includes information about the event, such as the location and time of occurrence. Some records include information about the suspect and victim.

Information

The purpose of this page is to display all the shooting incidents throughout the boroughs of NYC. It gives you information about all the shooting incidents.
For example, it gives you the case number, gives you where the shooting incident happened, the date of when it happened, and the time when it happened.

Analysis

This purpose of this page is the analyzation of this chart. The page allows the user to pick which dataset to display as a graph and filter the data by a specific value.
The graph on the left uses both datasets and filters the dataset by borough. The graph shows the number of shootings in each borough between 2006 to this current year.
